What are Art Salons?

Art Salons are creative collectives of 15 players who share a design aesthetic.

🏷️
Style tags define your salon
Each salon has three style tags that describe its aesthetic direction
⚖️
Tags guide voting
When voting, you'll see tags and judge how well designs match them — not compete against salonmates.
🎯
Weekly group goals
Complete challenges together to earn collective rewards.
